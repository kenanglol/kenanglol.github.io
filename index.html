Welcome

Coding

I first met coding in high school. In my early days, I approached him like a lesson. But as I dived deeper into it, I started to feel better and the meaning of coding changed for me. I think the software developer community has a rich culture and concept. Being surrounded by this community makes me feel happy.
Software development is one of the rare areas where you can come up with a complete product by working alone. This encourages me to develop software.

Listening

When I was a little kid, I didn't consciously listen to music. I was just being exposed to the music around. After high school, I realized that I love rock music. As I listened, I went to the past and met old school rock. During my college days, I started being selective when listening to music and focused on progressive rock.
I met Eloy when I was happy with Pink Floyd and King Crimson. This group pushed me to listen to Krautrock and Tangerine Dream invited me to electronic music world. I can define my current music taste as ambient and progressive rock.

Watching

I've been watching anime for 6 years. Like every kid, I started anime by watching Pokemon. But I didn't know it was an anime. My official entry into the anime world was with Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood. I continued to watch because I liked the unusual style that animes created with lines. Now I watch anime seasonally.
